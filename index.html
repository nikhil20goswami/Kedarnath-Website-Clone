About Kedarnath

Kedarnath Temple is one of the holiest Hindu shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva, nestled in the Himalayas.

How to Reach

  • By Air: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun
  • By Train: Rishikesh Railway Station
  • By Road: Drive or bus to Gaurikund, then trek 16 km

Travel Tips

  • Best time: May–June, Sept–Oct
  • Pack warm clothes and rain gear
  • Carry medicines and ID proof
